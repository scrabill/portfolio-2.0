Early Bird Registration

Airports Council International (ACI) is pleased to invite the Global Airport Industry to attend the 6th Annual ACI Airport Economics and Finance two-day conference and exhibition on Airport Investment, Financial Management and Economic Sustainability. This international event will take place at the exciting new venue in Singapore - the Carlton Hotel Singapore.

ACI Airport Economics and Finance Conference attracts a high level attendance by Airport CEO’s / Executive Directors, Airport CFO’s, Airport Property and Commercial Managers, Airport Investors and many more high level industry related executives.

This year’s event promises to deliver fresh and up to date topics to be discussed, analyzed and presented to all attendees. The conference theme is based upon ‘Looking into the Future”, with high level, industry leading conference presentations, discussing: 

- State of the Industry
- Economic Regulation
- Commercial Revenues & Customer Service
- Attracting & Maintaining Air Services

And many more key topics. For more information, contact events@aci.aero
