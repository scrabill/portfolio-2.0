PBS Branding & Media Kit

This conceptual rebranding was developed for the 40th anniversary of PBS: Public Broadcasting Service. The new, simplified logo visually represents what people today see as a “television” as well as alludes to their award winning online presence. Bright blues and greens were carried over from their existing brand to ease continuity.
